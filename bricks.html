Standard Brick

9×4.5×2.75 inches. Every brick is the same size — so your walls go up straight and your mortar stays consistent. Our most-used grade.

First Class Brick

Fired longer for a harder, denser brick. Very low water absorption and clean, sharp edges. Right for multi-storey buildings, columns, and exposed walls.

Heavy & Hollow Brick

For walls that need extra strength or better insulation. Available on larger orders. Ask us about minimum quantities and lead time.

How We Work

Clay to
Building Site.

From digging the clay to dropping bricks at your gate — the whole process, explained simply.

01

We Start With Good Clay

We source high-quality clay from controlled sites nearby. It's mixed and prepared until it has the right consistency for shaping.

02

We Shape Every Brick the Same

Clay goes through a machine press that cuts each brick to exactly the same size. Consistency here means easier building for you later.

03

We Fire Them Properly

Bricks go into our continuous kiln and are fired at 900–1100°C for 48 to 72 hours. Temperature is watched closely throughout for even strength.

04

We Check, Then Deliver

Once cooled, bricks are inspected, sorted by grade, and loaded onto trucks. Delivered directly to your site within the time we agreed.
