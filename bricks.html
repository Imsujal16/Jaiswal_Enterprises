Standard Brick

9×4.5×2.75 inches. Primary walling brick for residential construction. High clay density, clean corners. Our highest-volume output.

First Class Brick

Kiln-fired to full vitrification. Low water absorption, sharp edges. Specified for multi-storey structures, pillars, and exposed brickwork.

Heavy Duty / Hollow

Engineered for high-load applications and thermal-insulated wall systems. Available in bulk with minimum 10,000 unit orders.

02 — How We Work

Soil to
Structure.

From raw clay extraction to doorstep delivery — our process is tight, traceable, and built for scale.

01

Clay Sourcing & Preparation

High-plasticity alluvial clay is sourced from controlled deposits. Mixed and tempered for optimal consistency before moulding.

02

Machine Moulding

Wet clay pressed through mechanical extruders for dimensional accuracy. Cut to standard sizing with wire-gang cutters.

03

Kiln Firing

Green bricks are stacked and fired at 900–1100°C in our continuous kiln over 48–72 hours. Monitored for uniform heat penetration.

04

Quality & Dispatch

Cooled bricks are visually inspected, sorted by grade, stacked, and dispatched via truck directly to your site within the agreed window.
